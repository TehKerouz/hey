HOW TO DO THE 5.5.2 EXPLOIT TO LOAD SMASH MODS

 

PLEASE DON'T SKIP ANY STEPS

Step 1 - Setup your Smash Mods folders

Alright, so you will need to get those:
- SmashExplorer v0.07.2 : https://github.com/thefungus/Sm4shExplorer/releases
- SDCafiine v1.3 : https://www.wiiubru.com/appstore/zips/SDcafiine.zip
- Homebrew Launcher v1.4 : https://github.com/dimok789/homebrew_launcher/releases/download/1.4/homebrew_launcher.v1.4.zip
1) Put SDCafiine and the Homebrew Launcher on your SD Card.

You should get the folders setup like this (1) :
2) Next, create a /sdcafiine/ folder at the root of your SD Card. Get your folder with a lot of numbers in it, and put it inside of that folder. Then, go inside that folder with the numbers, and create a content folder in it. Then put every folder in content.
Here is a comparison between the 2 folder setups:


 

(There is also a way for you to have multiple modpacks, and you will be able to choose which one you want to load where loading SDCafiine. However I am not explaining it here, feel free to join the server linked at the bottom and @ me in #help so I can help you with that.)

s3) From now on, use SmashExplorer v0.07.2 to pack your mods. [I wrote a tutorial on how to update your SmashExplorer, if you want to check it out its right there.]

Step 2 - Launch mods

1) Launch the Wii U Internet Browser and clear the save data. (in the Settings, at the bottom, iirc)

2) Go to http://u.drg.li/.

3) Load a number. (apparently 2 is the one that works for most people)

4) If you get a white/grey screen but a freeze, reboot and then try that same number; it's the good one.
If not, reboot and try another number.

/!\ If nothing is working, you can try to replace the Homebrew Launcher with the 1.3 version; https://github.com/dimok789/homebrew_launcher/releases/download/v1.3/homebrew_launcher.v1.3.zip

5) Once you get into the Homebrew Launcher, launch the SDCafiine app you got in the Step 1.
You should be sent back into the Home Menu, launch Smash, and your mods should load.

(If you got a DS VC game to spare, or want a MUCH easier way to load mods, check out Haxchi! http://gamebanana.com/tuts/12402 )

 

Step 3 - Setup your new SmashExplorer

Get SmashExplorer v0.07.2 if you do not already have it: https://github.com/thefungus/Sm4shExplorer/releases
Go into your already existing SmashExplorer folder, who should look like this (this is my setup, yours might not be the same):


Take the Sm4shFileExplorer.exe file from the .zip you downloaded, and put it in your SmashExplorer folder. Say yes to replace the existing .exe file, and voil! You got updated to the new version of SmashExplorer.

 

Warning

Once you get everything setup, do not forget to setup your DNS! (thanks fungu for the og tutorial)

1) Go to the System Settings.
2) Go to "Internet".
3) Go to "Connect to the Internet".
4) Press "X" to show your current connections.
For the connection(s) you use, do the following:

Select the connection.
Select "Change Settings".
Move to the right and select "DNS".
Select "Don't Auto-obtain".
Of the two following addresses, set the closest to you as the Primary DNS, and the other as the Secondary DNS:

Los Angeles, USA: 168.235.092.108
Alblasserdam, NL: 081.004.127.020

Select "Confirm", then press "B" to save.
